✨ Happy Diwali ✨

Deepam

Deepam symbolizes the victory of light over darkness, illuminating homes during Diwali. Its flickering flames represent hope, unity, and the dispelling of negativity.

Crackers

Crackers add excitement to Diwali celebrations with their vibrant colors and sounds. They symbolize joy and the triumph of good over evil, bringing families together in festivity.

Sweets

Sweets are essential to Diwali, representing love and joy shared among family and friends. Exchanging these treats symbolizes the hope for prosperity and happiness in the coming year.
